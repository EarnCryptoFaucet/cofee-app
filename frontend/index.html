⚡ Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Shop

From ingredient tracking to sales analytics, BrewTrack has all the tools a growing coffee shop needs.

📦

Smart Inventory Tracking

Track every ingredient in real-time. Set thresholds and get instant alerts before you run out of critical stock.

🤖

Automatic Stock Deduction

When you make a sale, stock is automatically deducted based on your recipes. No manual counting required.

⚠️

Low Stock Alerts

Visual warnings and badges highlight ingredients that need restocking so you never run out mid-rush.

☕

Recipe Management

Define exact recipes for each product. BrewTrack uses them to automatically calculate ingredient usage per sale.

💰

Sales & Revenue Tracking

Record every sale with one click. View today's revenue, total orders, and sales history at a glance.

📊

Business Analytics

Visual charts showing top-selling products, daily revenue trends, and inventory health — all in one dashboard.

🚀 How It Works

Up & Running in 3 Simple Steps

No complicated setup. No training required. Just open the app and start managing your shop.

1

Add Your Ingredients

Input all your coffee ingredients — beans, milk, syrups, and more. Set quantities and low-stock thresholds.

2

Create Your Products

Add your menu items and define their recipes. Tell the system exactly what ingredients each product uses.

3

Sell & Track Instantly

Click "Sell" on any product. Stock is automatically deducted, sales are recorded, and revenue is updated.
